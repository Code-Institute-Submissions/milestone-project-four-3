About Us

Founded in 1925 by prominent archeologist Howard Carter, Artifact Auctioneers is a world renowned auction house in the selling of rare antiquities from ancient civilisation. With nearly one hundred years of expertise in finding long lost artifacts, our mission is to obtain not only the most prized and most sought after treasures, but also to make them purchasable as quickly as possible with minimum fuss.

Howard Carter

Howard Carter was was born in Kensington, London, on March 9, 1874, and the youngest of eight children, although he grew up in Swaffham, which was north of Norfolk, England. Carter had little formal education, but he was a very talented artist, and his principal study was that of art since his father was an illustrator. However, he wanted something different out of life.

He first stepped out into the field of archaeology in the fall of 1891, where he began his career with the Egyptian Exploration Fund as a tracer, where he copied drawings and inscriptions on paper for further studying. He was only 17 years old at the time.

He proved to be hardworking and faithful to his work, which led him into 31 years of digging and discovering before he found many treasures. One of these discoveries included one of the most valuable treasures ever uncovered - King Tut's tomb.

After retiring from field investigations Carter founded Artifact Auctioneers to bring archeological enthusiasts together from all over the world to share in his passion for artifacts.
